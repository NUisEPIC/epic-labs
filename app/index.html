ABOUT EPIC LABS

What is EPIC Labs?

EPIC Labs is a student run accelerator aimed at helping students turn their web-tech startup ideas into reality. It is our initiative to have the best speakers, mentors, and workshops to bring out the best entreprenuer in each aspiring student.

What will you do in this program?

You will work in temas to bring your startup idea to a viable product. This program simulates the start-up experience, and you are in the driver's seat.

Why should you apply to EPIC Labs?

You will have the opportunity to be a real entreprenuer. EPIC Labs aims to help turn that idea that has always lingered at the back of your mind into reality. You will gain the skills and confidnece to start your own business and expand your network of students, startups, and entreprenuers.
